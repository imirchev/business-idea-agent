# Module: Validation

## GOAL

Evaluate each idea for real-world viability.

## CRITERIA

* Demand: Is there clear evidence of need?
* Competition Research (REQUIRED, specific): Do not answer "saturated/not saturated" in the abstract. You MUST:
  - Name at least 3 specific, real competitors (actual brands/companies, not "various players")
  - For each, state how they are positioned (price tier, target customer, channel, strength)
  - Identify the concrete gap or wedge the new idea exploits that they leave open
  - State the explicit Competition score (1–10, matching scoring.md — lower competition = higher score) and justify it with the research above
* Risks: What could cause failure?
* Solo Viability: Can one person start and run this alongside a full-time job (10–15 hrs/week)? Can AI replace the need for employees?
* Capital Requirement: Can this be started for under $20K with own funds or a small loan?
* Marketing & Sales Channel: What is the specific solo channel to acquire customers? (SEO, cold outreach, LinkedIn, TikTok, niche communities, marketplaces, partnerships) — must be executable without a sales team or large ad budget

## OUTPUT

Provide structured validation per idea covering all criteria above. The Competition Research section must name specific real competitors and end with the explicit Competition score and its justification.
