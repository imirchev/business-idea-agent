# Module: Scoring

## GOAL

Score each idea objectively.

## SCORING DIMENSIONS (1–10)

* Demand — is there clear, proven market demand?
* Competition — lower competition = higher score
* Ease of Execution — how easy is it for ONE person to start and run alongside a full-time job using AI? Low complexity = high score. Score MUST be 7 or above or the idea is disqualified.
* Capital Fit — can it be started within the $5K–$20K budget (own money + small loan)? Closer to $5K and faster to first revenue = higher score.
* Profit Potential — revenue ceiling and margin quality
* Solo Marketing & Sales (AI-automatable) — how easily can ONE person acquire customers with NO sales/marketing team, using AI tools to do most of the work (content, outreach, lead-gen, follow-up)? Clear, low-cost, AI-automatable channel (SEO, cold email, LinkedIn, niche community, marketplace) = high score. If acquiring customers needs human selling at scale or an agency, score low. Score MUST be 7 or above or the idea is disqualified.

## DISQUALIFICATION RULE

Any idea scoring below 7 on EITHER Ease of Execution OR Solo Marketing & Sales is automatically disqualified. Replace it with a better-fit idea.

## OUTPUT

* Provide individual scores for all 6 dimensions
* Provide total score (out of 60)
* Flag any disqualified ideas and state why
